# GDPR / Privacy Law Compliance — Operational Checklist

## Verification by document type

### Employment / services contract
```
□ Purpose clause: what are the employee/contractor's data used for?
□ Legal basis declared (Art. 6 GDPR): contract execution or consent?
□ Data subject rights informed: access, rectification, erasure, objection, portability
□ Retention period stated
□ Third-party transfers: disclosed with safeguards?
□ Sensitive data: explicit written consent obtained?
```

### Privacy policy / terms of service
```
□ Controller identity and contact (DPO if required)
□ Specific purposes (not generic like "improve service")
□ Recipients of the data
□ International transfers and protection mechanisms
□ Retention period or criteria for determining it
□ Channel for exercising data rights
□ Right to withdraw consent
□ Existence of automated decision-making (if applicable)
```

### Data collection form
```
□ Clear consent text, no fine print
□ Separate checkbox for each purpose
□ No pre-checked boxes
□ Link to full privacy policy
□ Mandatory vs optional fields labelled
```

## Critical risks (require immediate correction)

| Situation | Article | Max penalty |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Processing without legal basis | Art. 6 GDPR | 4% global turnover |
| Failing to inform data subject | Art. 13-14 | 2% global turnover |
| No security measures | Art. 32 | 2% global turnover |
| Transfer without safeguards | Art. 46 | 4% global turnover |
| Sensitive data without explicit consent | Art. 9 | 4% global turnover |

```
FINDING: [brief description]

RISK: High / Medium / Low
FIX: [suggested text or concrete action]
```
